Crazy facts about red pandas

The red panda, also known as the "lesser panda", is an endangered species from Southwest China. The fact that the red panda was given the name "panda" about 50 years before the giant sexless panda should prove that the red panda is most definitely not a "lesser panda". Just like its gigantic wish.com knockoff the red panda eats primarily bamboo but will eat small mammals and insects on occasion. Some other facts about red pandas are:

  • They are very solitary creatures
  • They have the digestive system of a carnivore yet are primarily vegetarians
  • They are born covered in fur. Like these baby twins!

Introducing my wife to red pandas

My wife had never seen a red panda in her life and especially could never understand why I thought they are the cutest looking animal in the world. So I took her to "Brew at the Zoo" in Atlanta to introduce her. We had quite a few drinks by the time we got to the red panda exhibit and were quite saddened to find out they were extremely shy. We waited for about 40 mins and were about to leave when suddenly 1 came out to eat and lounge around.
My wife FREAKED out because it looked kinda like our dog but red and with a longer tail. Then she turned to me and in all seriousness said "Brandon, we have to steal it and take it home!" We came up with several dumb plans before realizing the red panda had gone back inside. However my wife was hooked on red pandas. Fun fact I have actually seen Red Pandas in multiple Countries:

  1. United States
    • Atlanta Zoo
    • San Antonio Zoo
    • Knoxville Zoo
  2. Japan
  3. South Korea
